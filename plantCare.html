Plant Care


Our primary goal is to provide support for every area of plant ownership by giving instruction through Plant Consultations and various events.

Stop by one of our store locations to get more information!

Step 1
(Maintain Distance)

- Ensure there is enough room (height and width) for the plant to grow

- The plant must have easy access to water so there should be enough distance among the leaves

Step 2
(Exposure to Light)

- Ensure that the plant is getting complete coverage from the sun

- Avoid parts of the plant from being blocked from sunlight

Step 3
(Water/Soil Moisture)

- Plan a Schedule for Watering Your Plants!

- Prevent any heat or AC vents to be near the plant as it will cause the leaves to blow away

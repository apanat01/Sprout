How do I place an order?

Browse through our available collection here, and give us a call at +1 (617) 608-3055 when you're ready!



Where can I pick up my order?

Orders can be picked up at any of our store locations.



What payment options do you offer?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, EBT, and Apple Pay.



My plant isn't doing well, what can I do?

Oh no, we're sorry to hear that! If it has been fewer than 14 days since you picked up your plant, bring it by any of our store locations and we'll replace or refund it. If it has been over 14 days, bring it by one of our stores and our expert team members will do our best to advise you on how to care for your plant. Or, take a look at our plant care page for some quick tips and tricks.

Meet the Team!

Ann Panat


Ann is a Sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Engineering Management. She is the Co-Founder here at Sprout. Throughout her time at Sprout, she has been determined in bringing plants into people’s lives, and providing a method for each customer to have the proper information necessary on how to care for specific plants. Outside of her love and admiration for Sprout, she enjoys playing tennis and dancing during her free time.

Angela is a plant enthusiast pursuing Studio Art and Engineering Psychology degrees at Tufts University.
